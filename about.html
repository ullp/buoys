About

Who We Are

A live electronic jamming collective based in Prague.

Buoys is a band born from a shared love for raw, unfiltered sound. We blend alternative rock with elements of indie, post-punk, and shoegaze to create something that feels both familiar and new.

Every show is a conversation — between the instruments, the silence, and the crowd. We don't just play music; we build moments. Whether in a packed club or a sweaty basement, we bring the same intensity.

Based in Prague, we're currently writing, recording, and shaping what comes next. If you believe in music that hits you in the chest, you're in the right place.

Buoys are about improvising and looking for the points where our waves come together. Uncertainty is the only thing you count on. We drift like loose buoys on waves.
